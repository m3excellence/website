About

Who we are

At New Horizons Recruitment d.o.o., we are dedicated to bridging the gap between skilled, semi-skilled, and non-skilled workers and industries across Europe. Our core mission is to provide reliable recruitment services to various sectors, including Construction, Delivery service, Printing & Packaging, Driving (Heavy and Light Vehicles), Electronics manufacturing sector, Welding (all technics), Facility management service, Hospitality management services, Telecom industry for installation operation and maintenance, Shipyard, Steel & Pipe fitter & OHS (Operational Health and Safety).

  • Delivering exceptional workforce solutions
  • Effective client communication
  • Exceed the expectations of our customers

We specialize in recruiting workers from Bangladesh, India, and Nepal, ensuring that each candidate is thoroughly vetted and trained to meet the highest industry standards. Our services include:

  • Recruitment of Skilled, Semi-Skilled, and Non-Skilled Workers
  • Work Permit and Visa Solutions: We offer efficient work permit visa services, ensuring a smooth transition for workers travelling abroad.
  • International Recruitment Solutions: We collaborate with leading industries in Europe to meet their workforce needs.
  • Comprehensive Training Programs: Our in-house M3 Centre of Excellence Training Center prepares workers for their jobs with hands-on training on modern equipment and skilled instructors.
  • Country-Specific Labor Law Training: We provide workers with detailed information on labour laws for their respective destination countries, ensuring compliance and smooth integration into their new roles.
